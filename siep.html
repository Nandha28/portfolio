SIEP E-BIKE CHALLENGE

We are proud to announce that our team emerged as the champions in the SIEP E-Bike Challenge held at Bhopal IES University.

ABOUT OUR TEAM

We were a team of 19 members including faculty members in Bhopal IES University. Despite facing numerous challenges, we showcased our determination, teamwork, and technical skills throughout the competition.
