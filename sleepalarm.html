ANTI-SLEEP ALARM FOR DRIVERS

Team Members: Four members from Karpagam College of Engineering collaborated on this project.

Institution:Karpagam College of Engineering

OVERVIEW

Objective:Develop an innovative system to enhance road safety by detecting driver drowsiness and alerting the driver.

Technology:Utilize Arduino microcontroller and ultrasonic sensor for real-time monitoring.

Key Component: Integration of a buzzer to provide immediate auditory alerts to the driver.

FUNCTIONALITY

  • The IR sensor continuously monitors the driver's eye movements and blinks.
  • When the system detects prolonged eye closure or irregular eye movements, it triggers the relay.
  • The relay, upon activation, signals the buzzer to emit a loud alarm, alerting the driver.

    • KEY FEATURES:

    Real-time Monitoring:Constantly analyzes driver behavior for signs of drowsiness such as head movements and eye closures.

    IR Sensor: Detects driver's eye movements and blinks, crucial indicators of drowsiness.

    Buzzer Alert:Activates a loud and distinctive sound to immediately alert the driver when drowsiness is detected.

    Customizable Alerts: The system can be configured to provide various alert types based on driver preferences and vehicle setup

    IMPACT AND BENEFITS:

    Enhanced Safety:Mitigates risks associated with driver fatigue, reducing the chances of accidents due to lapses in concentration.

    Prompt Intervention:Ensures drivers are alerted promptly, allowing them to take corrective actions or rest as needed.

    Innovative Solution:Showcases the application of technology in addressing critical road safety challenges.

    Scalability:Can be adapted for use in different vehicle types and driving conditions, contributing to broader safety initiatives.
